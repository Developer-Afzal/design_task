About Satnam Singh Sandhu

Hailed as a visionary educationist and philanthropist, Shri Satnam Singh Sandhu embodies the spirit of a new India where education, empowerment, and inclusivity drive national progress. Rising from humble beginnings in a farming family of Punjab, he has transformed higher education by building world-class institutions that serve the masses and empower lakhs of students across the country.
